Developing【开发】 TypeScript/Javascript on EOS!

作者： Lian

日期： 2018年05月25日 14:05

As a Block【区块】 Producer【开发者】, our main objective【目标】 is the adoption【采用】 of EOS in our community【社区】, and we know that one of the first【第一个】 barriers【障碍】 of entry【进入】 is the programming【编程】 language. It is our vision【希望】 that any programmer【开发者】 will be able to code【编程】 and deploy【发布】 smart【智能】 contracts【合约】 on EOS on their prefered【首选】 language.

In some cases, not knowing a complex【复杂】 language like c++ prevents【阻止】 developers from deploying【发布】 contracts on EOS, and is not due to lack【缺乏】 of capacity【能力】 or motivation【动机】, in general this is due to lack【缺少】 of time【时间】 that exists in this industry, where people consume【花费】 large【大量】 part of their hours going through libraries【库】 and frameworks【框架】 that they require for their daily【日常】 work, and can not often【经常】 afford to learn【学习】 other things.

So, how can we help【帮助】 developers that don’t currently【当前】 know c++ ?
There is huge【巨大】 potential【潜力】 in EOS, and in the use【使用】 of WebAssembly to decrease【减少】 this barrier【障碍】, and we want to take advantage【利用】 of it, we want the community【社区】 to benefit【受益】 from this opportunity.

In this sense, we have taken the time to modify【修改】 the Game of Life and recreate it in Typescript using【使用】 Assembly Script. For those of you that dont know【不知道】 Typescript, it is in short terms: “a typed superset【超集】 of Javascript”. Assembly Script allows【允许】 us to compile【编译】 Typescript in WebAssembly, Once【一旦】 compiled we can upload【上传】 this contract【合约】 in the EOS Blockchain. In other words, the use【使用】 of Typescript / Javascript in EOS will allow【允许】 incorporating the entire web community【社区】. It’s that great or not?

Our version【版本】 of Game of Life in Typscript can be found【找到】 in our github: https://github.com/EOSArgentina/eostypescript/tree/master/example. There you can find a step by step guide【指导】 on how to execute【执行】 our first TypeScript proof of concept【概念】.

You will be able to see the implementations【实现】 in TypeScript of:

Use of apply and execution of actions【行为】

Deserialization【去中心化】 of contract entry
parameters【参数】

Use of require_auth

Read【读取】 and Inserts【插入】 in tables

Same【相同】 abi file between c ++ and TypeScript

All these features【特性】 that we have implemented【实现】 are achieved through calls to EOSIO intrinsics, as a proof【证明】 of concept they have been grouped in a library / folder that we call【调用】 eoslib, which can be found【找到】 in the github code, please remember【记住】 that this It’s a first draft【草稿】 and it requires a lot of work and optimization【优化】. We hope everyone will join【加入】 us!
