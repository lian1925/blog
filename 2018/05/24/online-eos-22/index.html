Calling【呼唤】 all EOSIO Developers【开发者】

作者： Lian

日期： 2018年05月24日 08:05

Technical【技术】 Mentors【顾问】 Needed for the EOS Hackathon【马拉松】 in Hong Kong【香港】, June 9–10【六月9-10号】. Travel【旅行】 Stipends【薪资】 on Offer.

Can you spin up an EOSIO dev node【节点】 with your eyes closed? Do you know your way【方式】 around【围绕】 EOSIO’s C++ API? Do the words【单词】 ‘cleos’, ‘nodeos’ and ‘keosd’ mean【意味】 anything to you? Do you believe【相信】 that the future【未来】 is in DAPPs?

If your answer【答案】 to any of these questions【问题】 is ‘yes’ and you love contributing【贡献】 to the EOSIO community【社区】, then we want to hear from you!

We are looking for【寻找】 volunteers to be Technical Mentors【顾问】 at the EOS Global【全球】 Hackathon【马拉松】 in Hong Kong, June 9–10. Applicants【参与者】 who are accepted will receive travel expenses【费用】 to and from Hong Kong, as well as hotel【酒店】 expenses, up to the value of US$2,000【2000美元】.

In addition to being a vital【至关重要】 part to our kick-off hackathon, you’ll get to share【分享】 your knowledge【知识】 of the platform and help【帮助】 those who are trying to build【建立】 amazing and innovative【创新】 projects on EOSIO.

Teams from around the world are lined up to participate【参与】 in what will be the first event【事件】 in our EOS Global Hackathon series【系列】 — over the course of which we’ll be giving out US$1,500,000 in awards【奖励】 to the community. So, this is a great opportunity【机会】 to come and connect with the growing【成长】 global EOSIO development community!

If that prospect excites【兴奋】 you, please email【邮件】 info@eoshackathon.io with “volunteer mentor” in the subject【主题】 line and provide the following information:

Your name【名字】
City and country of residence【居住地】
Also tell us:

Why【原因】 you are interested in EOSIO
How well【了解程度】 you know EOSIO
What tech stacks【技术栈】 you are most proficient【精通】 in
If you have the right【合适】 credentials【证书】, we will respond with additional【额外】 information and details.

As a part of the Mentors team【团队】, you’ll be helping hackathon participants【参与者】 with:

Questions from new【新人】 EOSIO DAPP developers who may be getting stuck as they get started【开始】
Questions from mid-level【中级】 EOSIO DAPP developers who know the ropes but may not understand the best tools, easiest shortcuts, or the right solution for a hack build

General tech troubleshooting【障碍】 questions. We’re not saying you’re gonna be asked to help connect to the wifi… but you might be!

Eating pizza【披萨】, shooting【射击】 nerf guns and having fun【快乐】 :)
We look forward to hearing from you!
