Lesson 18 He often does this!

Lesson 18 He often does this! 他经常干这种事

提问

What had happened to the writer’s bag？

课文

After I had had lunch at a village pub1, I looked for my bag.

I had left it on chair beside the door and now it wasn’t there!

As I was looking for it, thlandlord2 came in.

‘Did you have a good meal?” he asked.

‘Yes, thank you,’ I answered, ‘but I can’t pay the bill. I haven’t got my bag.’

The landlord smiled and immediately3 went out.

In a few minutes he returned with mbag and gave it back to me.

‘I’m very sorry,’ he said. ‘My dog had taken it into the garden.

He often does this!

生词和短语

pub n. 小酒店
landlord n. 店主
bill n. 帐单

参考译文

我在一家乡村小酒店吃过午饭后，就找我的提包。

我曾把它放在门边的椅子上，可这会儿不见了！

当我正在寻找酒店老板走了进来。
“您吃得好吗？”他问。

“很好，谢谢。”我回答，“但我付不了帐，我的提包没有了。”

酒店老板笑了笑，马上走了出去。

一会儿工夫他拿着我的提包回来了，把它还给了我。

“实在抱歉，”他说，“我的狗把它弄到花园里去了，他常干这种事！”
