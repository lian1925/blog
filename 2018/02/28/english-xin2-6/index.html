lesson 6 Percy Buttons

课文

I have just moved to a house in BridgeStreet.

Yesterday a beggar1 knocked at mydoor.

He asked me for a meal and a glass of beer.

In return for this, the beggarstood on his head and sang songs.

I gavehim a meal. He ate the food and drank the beer.

Then he put a piece of cheesein his pocket and went away.

Later aneighbour told me about him.

Everybodyknows him. His name is Percy Buttons.

He calls at every house in the street once a month and always asks for a meal and aglass of beer.

生词和短语

beggar n. 乞丐
food n. 食物
pocket n. 衣服口袋
call v. 拜访，光顾

##参考译文

我刚刚搬进了大桥街的一所房子。

昨天一个乞丐来敲我的门，问我要一顿饭和一杯啤酒。

作为回报，那乞丐头顶地倒立起来，嘴里还唱着歌。

我给了他一顿饭。他把食物吃完，又喝了酒。然后把一块乳酪装进衣袋里走了。

后来，一位邻居告诉了我他的情况。

大家都认识他，他叫珀西.巴顿斯。他每月对这条街上的每户人家光顾一次，总是请求给他一顿饭和一杯啤酒。

(完)
